Identity Coaching

Strategy is the map, but you are the driver. Embody your brand voice with these core coaching principles.

Embodiment Phase

Own Your Unique Narrative

Don't shy away from parts of your story that feel 'too personal.' Your specific experiences and wins are your greatest competitive advantage over AI-generated filler.

Consistency Is Not Just Visual

Colours create recognition, but 'vibe' creates trust. Ensure the way you speak in captions matches the way you speak in a DM or discovery call.

Identify Your 'Non-Negotiables'

What does your brand stand against? Clear boundaries make content creation easier because you aren’t trying to please everyone—only your tribe.

Practice 'Active Reflection'

Before hitting post, ask: Does this sound like me, or who I think I should be? Peel back industry standards to find your actual voice.

Document, Don't Just Create

Identity is found in the 'behind-the-scenes.' Share the messy desk, the research phase, or the morning coffee to humanise your strategy.

The 80/20 Rule of Authority

80% value and expertise, 20% personality. This balance ensures you are seen as a professional while remaining someone people want to work with.

The Storytelling Framework

Don't just record content—deploy your professional identity using our proven three-phase structure.

Authenticity

Clients trust experts, not actors. Small imperfections make you more relatable and trustworthy.

Phase 1: The Hook

Open with a specific problem or question your ideal client is facing today.

Phase 2: The Core Insight

Deliver your "Prime" solution. Keep it concise, high-value, and easy to digest.

Phase 3: The Conversion

Give them a clear next step. "Follow for more insights" or "Book a consultation."
